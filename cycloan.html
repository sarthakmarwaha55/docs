Cycloan

Cycles offer an easy and efficient way to travel short distances especially last mile journeys where public transport may not be available and traditional ride-sharing services may not be an option. However, owing a cycle is expensive and dangerous as safe storage and proper maintenance makes it hard to justify the investment. Cycloan aims to solve this problem by allowing users to rent cycles from various cycle stations/hubs for short journeys. Once the user has completed this journey, the cycle can be deposited back to any hub/station. Such a platform should encourage exercise and also reduce the traffic on the roads by making public transport a more viable option for places lacking last mile connectivity.
